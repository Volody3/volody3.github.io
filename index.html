Mike Vazovsky

Mike Wazowski is a short green, round monster with a large head, main body, arms and legs. On the head there are small horns and one large widened eye with a blue iris in the middle of the head.


As a child, Mike was a simple elementary school student; he had no friends, and none of his classmates paid attention to him. One day, he and his class went on a tour of Monsters, Inc. Mike, violating safety rules, fights off the main crowd, watches the work of one of the scarecrows - Frank McKay. The one, surprised by Mike's noiselessness, strongly recommends him to enter the University of Monsters, seeing in him great potential.

Randall Boggs

The main villain of the Pixar animated feature film "Monsters, Inc." in 2001, and later - one of the minor antagonists of its 2013 prequel to "Monsters University". He is the sworn enemy of James Sullivan and Mike Wazowski.


At the beginning of Monsters University, Randall has a timid and insecure personality; nevertheless, realizing this, he tries to join the circle of "cool parties", and after a while he succeeds, joining the brotherhood of "Roar of Hoarse Mouths". During the last test of the Scary Games, Randall fails it miserably because of Sally, after which he begins to harbor anger at him for the rest of his life, promising himself "never to lose to Sullivan again."

Boo
